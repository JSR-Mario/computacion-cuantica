\textbf{Calcule la matriz densidad $\rho (t) = \ket{\psi (t)} \bra{\psi (t)}$} \vspace{.3cm}

Tenemos:
$$
\ket{+} = \frac{\ket{0}+\ket{1}}{\sqrt{2}}, \ \ \ket{\psi_0} =
\frac{1}{2}(\ket{00}+\ket{01}+\ket{10}+\ket{11})
$$

Recordamos ademas que:
$$
Z \ket{0} = 1 \ket{0}, \ \ \ Z \ket{1} = -1 \ket{1}
$$

De aqui sale que:
\begin{align*}
    \ket{00} &= 1*1 = 1 \\
    \ket{01} &= 1*(-1) = -1 \\
    \ket{10} &= (-1)*1 = -1 \\
    \ket{00} &= 1*1 = 1 \\
\end{align*}

Por tanto:
\begin{align*}
    U(t)\ket{00} &= e^{-it} \ket{00}, \\
    U(t)\ket{11} &= e^{-it} \ket{11}, \\
    U(t)\ket{01} &= e^{it} \ket{01}, \\
    U(t)\ket{10} &= e^{it} \ket{10}
\end{align*}

El estado en el tiempo t: 
$$
\ket{\psi(t)} = \frac{1}{2} (e^{-it}\ket{00}+e^{it}\ket{01}+e^{it}\ket{10}+e^{-it}\ket{11})
$$

Ahora, tenemos que:
$$
e^{\pm 2it} = cos(2t) \pm i \ sin (2t) 
$$

$$\frac{1}{4}
\begin{pmatrix}
    1 & cos(2t)-i \ sin(2t) & cos(2t)-i \ sin(2t) & 1\\   
    cos(2t)+i \ sin(2t) & 1 & 1 & cos(2t)+i \ sin(2t)\\   
    cos(2t)+i \ sin(2t) & 1 & 1 & cos(2t)+i \ sin(2t)\\   
    1 & cos(2t)-i \ sin(2t) & cos(2t)-i \ sin(2t) & 1\\   
\end{pmatrix}
$$
